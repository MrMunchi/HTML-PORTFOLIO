*****'s Favorite Christmas Movies

Top 4 Christmas Movies Of All Time*

1. Home Alone (1990)

This movie is one of the greatest materpieces in history. The jokes are funny, the plot is amazing, and it is overall one of my favorite movies

2. Unaccompanied Minors (2006)

This movie is about a group of kids who are traveling alone, and they meet in an airport on christmas, and they get locked in a room with all the kids. They escape, and decorate the whole airport for christmas. It's overall a great movie.

3.Rudolph The Red-Nosed Reindeer (1964)

Everybody knows the nursery rhyme, and we all have probably seen the movie, but it is such a funny, and charming movie, it makes the list.

4.Frosty The Snowman (1969)

This is the true OG. One of the greatest christmas movies of all time, the storytelling is amazing, and you can tell a lot of effort and care went into this movie.

*Not ranked best to worst, or favorite to least favorite
